Protos is a company originally created in 2213 by five innovators willing to help people.

Among us are a bunch of doctors and biology experts. We’ve noticed that even now, in a Time when we have greatly improved our mastery of technology, coming across a person missing a limb or an eye in the street is not that rare. And we thought : “ How? Can’t we fix that? Can’t them? “, and here we are. Several years later, we managed to create and popularize bionic organs to help the whole population.

We are very happy to have come this far already, but we are host to a much greater ambition. The world evolves, and we want the human beings to evolve along with it.
